Dun Zeng (曾趸)

Hi there! I am currently a Ph.D. candidate at the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC). I am very fortunately supervised by Prof. Zenglin Xu. Before that, I received my Bachelor’s Degree from Ocean University of China (OUC) in 2020.

My research interests primarily focus on Distributed Optimization and Federated Learning. I work on solving the challenge of conflicting optimization objectives in machine learning models, thereby improving the efficiency of data application in human society. Recently, I focused on designing, analyzing, and evaluating federated optimization algorithms, considering communication and heterogeneity to achieve a high generalization model in federated learning.

I am pursuing academic collaborations, particularly in the field of distributed optimization. Please feel free to reach out!
